What actually matters

  • Infra will not block you — even at 200 shops vendors are ~13% of ARR.
  • Security spend is small early — Clerk + TLS + Neon backups; add Sentry/uptime by ~10–25 shops; pen-test/SOC2 projects later (~$6–18k/yr).
  • Mobile is an AI Plus COGS line (~$9→$55/mo platform) plus Gemini per seat — customers pay $20/mo for the bundle.
  • People cost is the real gate — full 4‑person payroll on $172k ARR loses money; stay founder-lean until attach/price expand.
